01 Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve Foodoo. Review this list and remove anything Foodoo does not collect.

Information you provide

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile details — dietary preferences, saved addresses, and favorites.
  • Order information — items you order, special instructions, and order history.
  • Payment information — handled securely by our payment processor; we do not store full card numbers.
  • Communications — messages you send to support, reviews, and feedback.

Information collected automatically

  • Device information — device model, operating system, app version, and unique identifiers.
  • Usage data — features used, screens viewed, searches, and interaction patterns.
  • Location data — to show nearby restaurants and enable delivery (see Section 04).
  • Diagnostics — crash logs and performance data to keep the App running smoothly.

02 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account and process your orders.
  • Connect you with restaurants and delivery partners.
  • Show relevant restaurants, menus, and recommendations near you.
  • Process payments and send order confirmations and receipts.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Send service updates and, where permitted, promotional messages you can opt out of.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues.
  • Improve and develop new features, and comply with legal obligations.

03 How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed to operate Foodoo:

  • Restaurants — order details so your food can be prepared.
  • Delivery partners — your name, delivery address, and order so it can be delivered.
  • Payment processors — to securely complete transactions.
  • Service providers — vendors who help us with hosting, analytics, and support, under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal reasons — when required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of users and the public.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

04 Location Data

With your permission, Foodoo uses your device’s location to find nearby restaurants, estimate delivery times, and provide accurate delivery. You can enable or disable location access at any time in your device settings. Disabling location may limit some features, such as automatic address detection.

05 Third-Party Services

Foodoo relies on third-party services that may process limited data under their own privacy policies. These may include [e.g., Apple, Google Maps, Stripe, Firebase]. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through the App.

06 Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

07 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent or opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at phvzed@gmail.com. You can also manage account details and notification preferences directly in the App.

08 Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we work to safeguard your data.

09 Children’s Privacy

Foodoo is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App. Your continued use of Foodoo after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
